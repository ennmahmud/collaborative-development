FAQ Interface

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to the Open Day?

We recommend bringing:

  • Comfortable shoes for walking around campus
  • Your registration confirmation (digital or printed)
  • A notepad and pen for taking notes
  • A bottle of water
  • Questions you'd like to ask staff and students
How do I get to the University?

The University of Wolves City Campus is located in the heart of Wolverhampton city center. You can reach us by:

  • Train: Wolves train station is a 10-minute walk from campus
  • Bus: Multiple bus routes stop near the campus
  • Car: Limited parking is available on campus (pre-booking required)

Detailed directions can be found in the "Getting Here" section of this app.

Can I bring family members to the Open Day?

Yes, absolutely! We encourage you to bring family members or friends who may be helping you with your university decision. All visitors should be registered in advance so we can plan accordingly.

Will I be able to see accommodation during the Open Day?

Yes, we offer tours of our university accommodation during all Open Days. These tours run regularly throughout the day and can be booked at the Accommodation stand when you arrive, or you can add them to your personal agenda in advance using this app.

How can I change or cancel my Open Day registration?

You can modify or cancel your registration by logging into your account on this app and visiting the "My Bookings" section. Alternatively, you can contact our Open Day team at openday@wlv.ac.uk or call 01902 321000.
